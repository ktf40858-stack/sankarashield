About Sankarashield

Pioneering Cybersecurity Excellence Since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Sankarashield emerged from a vision to democratize enterprise-grade cybersecurity solutions for businesses of all sizes. Named after Thomas Sankara's revolutionary spirit of independence and progress, we embody the same dedication to empowering organizations with cutting-edge network security.

As a young, dynamic company with a team of passionate cybersecurity experts, we've quickly established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses seeking robust, affordable, and scalable security solutions. Our growth from a small startup to a recognized player in the cybersecurity landscape reflects our commitment to excellence and innovation.

Despite being a relatively new entrant, we've successfully deployed over 50 security solutions, protecting more than 20 active clients across various industries. Our agile approach allows us to adapt quickly to emerging threats while maintaining the personalized service that larger corporations often lack.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, accessible, and reliable cybersecurity solutions that empower businesses to thrive in the digital age without compromising on security. We believe that every organization, regardless of size, deserves enterprise-level protection.

Our Vision

To become the leading cybersecurity partner for emerging businesses across Africa and beyond, setting new standards for network security, accessibility, and customer service in the digital transformation era.
